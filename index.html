The apps

✸

Messenger

Private real-time messaging, fully on-chain

A direct, real-time messenger that lives inside your own canister. Conversations, contacts and presence run on the Internet Computer — there is no central server relaying your messages. Mutual-contact handshakes keep your inbox yours, and the whole frontend is reproducible and verifiable from source.

  • One-to-one messaging with presence and contact requests
  • Runs in a canister you control — no backend to trust
  • In-app #verify page proves the running code matches the public source
⛁

Vault

End-to-end encrypted file storage you own

A personal encrypted vault for your files. Content is end-to-end encrypted with VetKeys, so only you can read it — the canister stores ciphertext it can never open. Vault is also self-upgradeable: you review and apply updates on your own terms, with rollback if you change your mind.

  • End-to-end encryption (VetKeys) — one key per category, envelope-sealed
  • Self-upgrade from signed, reproducible releases — with rollback
  • Your files, your keys, your canister: nobody else holds the plaintext
