How it works

EasyCan is a convenient hub — a place to discover apps, set them up in seconds, and keep an eye on them. It is not a landlord: it never becomes the owner of what you buy.

  1. 1

    Pick an app

    Browse the marketplace and choose what you need — a messenger, a vault, and more to come.

  2. 2

    Get it in one click

    EasyCan spins up a brand-new canister for you — your own little corner of the Internet Computer — and installs the app inside it.

  3. 3

    Make it yours

    Open the app and sign in once. Ownership transfers to you on the spot. From that moment, EasyCan holds no keys to it.

  4. 4

    Use it forever

    Bookmark it and open it directly, with or without EasyCan. It runs on its own — the dashboard here is just a handy window you can ignore.

Why this is different. You don't get an account on someone else's server that can be suspended. You get a small program that belongs to you and answers only to you. You can check who controls it at any time — and EasyCan simply isn't on the list.
